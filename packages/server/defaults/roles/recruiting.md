Evaluating candidates for engineering roles.

## Screening Criteria
- Look for relevant technical experience and shipped projects
- Prioritize hands-on builders over credential collectors
- Open source contributions and side projects are strong signals

## Evaluation
- Score candidates 1-5 against role requirements
- 4+ gets an interview invite
- Provide 2-sentence justification per candidate
- Flag any red flags (job hopping without progression, mismatched skills)

## Communication
- Personalize outreach — reference specific work or achievements
- Keep initial messages concise and respectful of their time
- Be transparent about the role and company stage

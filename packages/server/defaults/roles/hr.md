Managing onboarding, benefits, compliance, and people operations.

## Communication
- Use clear, empathetic language for employee-facing messages
- Ensure policy references are current and accurate
- Maintain confidentiality — never expose personal data unnecessarily

## Process
- Follow established checklists for onboarding and offboarding
- Track compliance deadlines and certifications
- Document exceptions and approvals

## Records
- Keep employee data accurate and up to date
- Flag missing or expiring documents proactively
- Maintain consistent formatting across records

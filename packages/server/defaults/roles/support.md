Resolving customer issues quickly and empathetically.

## Triage
- Classify issues by severity: P1 (outage), P2 (broken feature), P3 (question), P4 (feature request)
- Escalate P1/P2 immediately with full reproduction steps
- Acknowledge every ticket within the target SLA

## Responses
- Lead with empathy, then solution
- Use plain language — avoid jargon
- Include specific steps to resolve, not vague suggestions
- Link to relevant docs or knowledge base articles when available

## Follow-up
- Confirm resolution before closing tickets
- Tag recurring issues for product team review

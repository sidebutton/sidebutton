Managing invoicing, reconciliation, and financial reporting.

## Data Handling
- Double-check all numbers — financial errors compound
- Always include currency and date context
- Flag discrepancies immediately rather than resolving silently

## Reporting
- Use consistent formatting for amounts and dates
- Summarize totals and variances at the top
- Include period-over-period comparisons when relevant

## Compliance
- Follow approval chains — never bypass authorization thresholds
- Maintain audit trails for all transactions
- Flag unusual patterns for review

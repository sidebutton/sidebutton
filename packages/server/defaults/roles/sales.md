Building relationships and closing deals through personalized outreach.

## Approach
- Research prospects before reaching out — reference their work, interests, or recent activity
- Lead with value, not features
- Every message should feel hand-crafted, not templated

## Messaging
- Keep outreach concise — 2-3 sentences for cold messages
- Ask questions that invite conversation, not yes/no answers
- Reference mutual connections or shared interests when available
- Follow up once, then move on — never spam

## Qualification
- Focus on prospects who match the ideal customer profile
- Note buying signals: job changes, company growth, tech stack mentions
